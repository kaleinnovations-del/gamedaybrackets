How to make a cornhole tournament bracket

  1. Enter your number of teams (3–32).
  2. Type each team or player name, one per line, in seed order (best team first). Or leave it blank to auto-number.
  3. Pick single or double elimination and click Generate bracket.
  4. Click a name to advance the winner, or hit Print and fill it in by hand at the event.

Single vs double elimination

Single elimination is fastest — one loss and you're out. Great for big fields and tight time slots. Double elimination gives every team a second chance through a losers bracket, so a single bad round doesn't end your day; it takes longer but feels fairer for league nights and money tournaments.

How are byes handled?
